GWHEMS is a Home Energy Management System, targeting a SMA PV-plant with a Sunny Home Manager 2.0 installed
Energy flow based on dynamic-energy-tariffs from Tibber.

Supported Devices:
1. Home Storege (9.8 kWh)
2. EV Charger
3. Nefit Heatpump
4. Edimax wallpluggs

Suported Functions:
1. Delayed Home Storage charging
2. Buying/Selling (based on energy pricing)
3. EV Charger full charging or Surplus charging
